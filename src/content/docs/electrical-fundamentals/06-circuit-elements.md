Two types of circuit elements.

- Active
- Passive

## Active

Capable of generating electrical energy.

- Voltage sources
- Current sources

These can interchangeably be used.

## Passive

Either consumes or stores electrical energy.

- Resistors
- Inductors
- Capacitors
- Any other elements
