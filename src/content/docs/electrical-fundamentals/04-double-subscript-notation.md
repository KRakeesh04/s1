---
title: Double subscript notation
---

## Current

```math
i_{ab} = - i_{ba}
```

When current is flowing **from point $ a $ to point $ b $**, it's noted as
above.

## Voltage

```math
v_{ab} = -v_{ba} = v_a - v_b
```

When voltage is **higher at point $ a $ and lower at point $ b $**, it's notes
as above.
