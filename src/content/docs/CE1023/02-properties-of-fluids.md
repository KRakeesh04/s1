## Mass Density

$$ \rho = \frac{\text{Mass}}{\text{Volume}} = \frac{m}{v} $$

### At a point

```math
\rho = \lim_{\delta v \to 0} \frac{\delta m}{\delta v}
```

## Dimensions

| Property     | Dimension     | Units          |
| ------------ | ------------- | -------------- |
| Mass Density | $$ ML^{-3} $$ | $$ kgm^{-3} $$ |
