A rolled up sheet of graphene.

Properties:

- Extraordinary electrical and heat conductivity
- High mechanical strength

## Classifications

Based on **\_\_**:

- Single wall carbon nanotubes (SWNT)
- Multi wall carbon nanotubes (MWNT)
