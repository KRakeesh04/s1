Matrix obtained from a given matrix by interchanging its rows and columns.
Denoted by a superscript T, like $A^T$. $ $

## Properties

1. $(A^T)^T=A$
2. Distributive over addition: $(A+B)^T=A^T+B^T$
3. $(kA)^T=kA^T$
4. $(A\times B)^T=B^T\times A^T$
