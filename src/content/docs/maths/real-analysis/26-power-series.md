A series of the form:

```math
\sum_{n=0}^\infty
a_n (x-c)^n
```

Here:

- $x$ - a variable
- $c$ - a constant

## Radius of convergence

Maximum radius of $x$ in where the series converges. $ $

```math
R = \sup{\big\{r\;|\; \text{series converges for}\; |x-c| < r\big\}}
```

## Range of convergence

$(c-R,c+R)$ is the range of convergence. $ $
