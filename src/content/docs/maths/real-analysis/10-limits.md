$\lim_\limits{x\to{a}}{f(x)}=L$ **iff**: $ $

```math
\forall{\epsilon>0}\;
\exists{\delta>0}\;
\forall{x}\;
(0<|x-a|<\delta\implies{|f(x)-L|<\epsilon})
```

Defining $\delta$ in terms of a given $\epsilon$ is enough to prove a limit.
