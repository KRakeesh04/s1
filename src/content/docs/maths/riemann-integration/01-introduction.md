## Interval

Let $I=[a,b]$. Length of the interval $\lvert{I}\rvert=b-a$.

## Disjoint interval

When 2 intervals don't share any common numbers.

## Almost disjoint interval

When 2 intervals are disjoint or intersect only at a common endpoint.
